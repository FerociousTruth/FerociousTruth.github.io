Authors

J Thomas Moros

J is an aspiring rationalist who has been involved in the Columbus Rationality community since January 2016. His rapid deconversion from intelligent Christian in 2013 gives him a unique perspective. His degree in computer science with a minor in math, and subsequent successful career as a computer programmer have given him the foundation of knowledge to tackle even the most technical of subjects.

He started the Ferocious Truth blog in order to explore interesting topics with a rationalist approach. Topics that interest him include philosophy, mathematics, artificial intelligence, the nature of reality and rationality itself.
